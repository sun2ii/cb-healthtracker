# CB HealthTracker - User Guide

Welcome, Margaret! This guide will help you use CB HealthTracker to manage your daily health routine.

## Getting Started

When you open the app, you'll see your **Home** screen with:
- Your profile picture and a greeting
- Quick stats (Check-in status, Streak, Medications count)
- Your medications list
- Recent activity
- Upcoming appointments (coming soon!)

---

## Daily Check-In

**What it does:** Let your family know you're doing okay each day.

**How to use:**
1. Tap the **Check-in** card on the home screen (or tap "Check-in" in the navigation)
2. Select how you're feeling: **"I'm Okay"** or **"Not Feeling Well"**
3. Optionally add a note about how you're doing
4. Tap **Submit**

**Tip:** Check in every day to build your streak! Your family can see when you've checked in.

---

## Managing Medications

### Viewing Your Medications
- From Home, tap **"View"** on the Medications card
- Or tap **"Meds"** in the bottom navigation

### Adding a New Medication
1. Tap **"Add Med"** button (on Home or Medications page)
2. Fill in:
   - **Name** (e.g., "Metformin")
   - **Dose** (e.g., "500mg")
   - **Frequency** (e.g., "Twice daily")
   - **Time of day** (e.g., "Morning", "With meals")
   - **Instructions** (optional, e.g., "Take with food")
3. Tap **Save**

### Removing a Medication
1. Go to the Medications page
2. Find the medication you want to remove
3. Tap the **trash icon** on the right side

---

## Viewing Your Activity History

**What it shows:** Your past check-ins and medication logs organized by day.

**How to use:**
1. Tap **"History"** on the home screen, or tap **"Activity"** in the navigation
2. Browse through your daily activity
3. Tap on a day to see details in a popup

---

## Your Profile

### Viewing Your Profile
Tap **"Profile"** in the navigation to see:
- Your name and photo
- Age
- Medical conditions
- Emergency contact information

### Editing Your Profile
1. Go to Profile page
2. Tap **"Edit"**
3. Update your information
4. Tap **Save**
